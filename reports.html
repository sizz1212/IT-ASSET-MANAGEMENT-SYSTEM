Reports

Generate and export detailed reports on assets, assignments, and maintenance.

Asset Report

A full inventory snapshot — all assets, their categories, condition, acquisition cost, and current status.

  • Asset ID, name, and category
  • Acquisition date and cost
  • Current condition and location
  • Depreciation summary

Assignment Report

Track which assets are assigned to whom, for how long, and identify unassigned or overdue returns.

  • Assigned asset and assignee
  • Assignment and return dates
  • Department and location
  • Overdue and unassigned assets

Maintenance Report

A history of all maintenance activities — completed, in-progress, and pending — with cost and time data.

  • Maintenance type and description
  • Scheduled vs. actual dates
  • Status and assigned technician
  • Cost and duration breakdown
